## 🏢 About Us

**NarneTech Software Solutions** is a technology company based in Vijayawada, India, helping businesses modernise the way they run. We bring full-stack software engineering, industrial IoT, and digital marketing together under one roof — so a company can go from a spreadsheet to a connected, AI-assisted operation with a single partner.

We design, build, deploy, and maintain end-to-end systems: web platforms, enterprise applications, IoT gateways on the factory floor, and the cloud infrastructure that ties them together.

## 🚀 Flagship Product — PlantPulse

> **AI-powered Industrial EMS + CMMS platform**

PlantPulse turns raw machine and energy data into actionable intelligence for industrial plants — combining real-time **Energy Management** with **Computerised Maintenance Management** in one platform.

- 📊 Live energy monitoring via on-site ESP32 / Modbus IoT gateways
- 🔧 Maintenance scheduling, breakdown tracking, and downtime analytics
- 🤖 AI-driven insights, anomaly detection, and savings recommendations
- 🏭 Built for **foundries, manufacturing, cement, food processing, and pharma**
